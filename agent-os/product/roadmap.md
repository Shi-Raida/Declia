# Declia — Product Roadmap

## V1 — Launch

Core platform covering the full photographer workflow end-to-end.

**Public-facing:**
- Vitrine publique (SEO, portfolio, service presentation)
- Reservation system (sessions, mini-sessions, module scolaire)
- Client space & galleries (livraison sécurisée, sélection photos)
- Before/after retouche preview
- Boutique intégrée (tirages, objets photo, coffret numérique)
- Cartes cadeaux
- Fidélité & parrainage (points, referral)
- Offres promotionnelles

**Marketing automation V1:**
- Avant-Première (early access campaign before gallery release)
- Panier abandonné (abandoned cart recovery)
- Dernière chance (last-call before gallery expiry)
- Réveil galerie (re-engagement for inactive clients)
- Session recap (post-session summary email)
- Referral proof (social proof via client referrals)

**Communications:**
- Emails automatisés (transactionnel + marketing)
- SMS automatisés
- Education hub (client-facing content)

**Back-office:**
- CRM (client profiles, historique, notes)
- Planning & agenda (sessions, disponibilités)
- Workflow automation (tâches automatiques par étape)
- Facturation (Factur-X, devis, factures)
- Export comptable
- Statistiques & reporting
- Notifications (admin alerts)

---

## V1.5 — Consolidation

_Target: 2–4 months post-launch_

Deepening the product with collaborative, SEO, and advanced marketing features.

- **Mood board collaboratif** — client shares visual inspiration before session
- **Sélection guidée intelligente** — assisted photo selection experience
- **Album proofing** — client review and approval of album layouts
- **Gift flow immersif** — enhanced gift card purchase and redemption experience
- **Réseau prestataires** — directory/referral network (makeup artists, venues, etc.)
- **Blog SEO intégré** — native blog for organic search traffic
- **Météo intelligente** — weather-aware scheduling suggestions
- **Campagnes avancées:**
  - Anniversaire client (birthday campaigns)
  - Upsell post-livraison
  - Campagnes saisonnières (Noël, Saint-Valentin, rentrée…)
- **Témoignage vidéo** — video testimonial collection from clients
- **Revenue forecast** — projected revenue dashboard for the photographer

---

## V2 — Innovation IA

_Target: 4–8 months post-launch_

AI-powered features that create competitive moat and premium positioning.

- **Reconnaissance faciale** — automatic tagging and client-facing face search in gallery
- **Memory Vault** — long-term archiving service (paid storage subscription)
- **Print preview AR** — augmented reality preview of prints on client's walls
- **Retouche & tirage sans shooting** — AI-enhanced products from existing archives
- **Intégration Lightroom & Capture One** — direct plugin sync for photographer workflow
- **Smart pricing** — AI-suggested pricing based on demand, season, and market data
- **Pack réseaux sociaux + planning éditorial IA** — auto-generated social media content from delivered photos
- **Style consistency check** — AI review to ensure gallery edits are stylistically consistent
